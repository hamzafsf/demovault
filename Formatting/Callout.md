> [!FAQ]- Are callouts foldable?

- abstract, summary, tldr
- info, todo
- tip, hint, important
- success, check, done
- question, help, faq
- warning, caution, attention
- failure, fail, missing
- danger, error
- bug
- example
- quote, cite
